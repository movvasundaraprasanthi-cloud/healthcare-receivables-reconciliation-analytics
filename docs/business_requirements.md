# Business Requirements Document

## Business Theme
Healthcare prescription receivables, unapplied cash, claims/cash posting, reconciliation, and client reporting

## Objective
Deliver a recruiter-ready analytics package with synthetic business data, SQL transformations, Python validation, BI-ready exports, and stakeholder documentation.

## Functional Requirements
- Load source extracts from `data/raw/`.
- Validate required fields and business rules.
- Produce processed datasets in `data/processed/`.
- Produce dashboard-ready CSVs in `data/exports/`.
- Document KPIs, assumptions, validation checks, and stakeholder insights.

## Data Safety
All data is synthetic. No PHI, patient names, SSNs, DOBs, private customer data, or production data is included.
